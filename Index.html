About Us

Nagaad Cafe & Restaurant, located at Sanca Intersection in Yaaqshiid District, Mogadishu, is a warm and welcoming destination that combines authentic Somali flavors with exceptional service. We take pride in preparing traditional meals and delightful desserts with care, ensuring every guest experiences the true taste of Somalia. Beyond dining, Nagaad serves as the perfect venue for family gatherings, friendly hangouts, and special celebrations such as weddings and events, offering an inviting atmosphere where hospitality and community come together. At Nagaad Cafe & Restaurant, flavor meets quality, and every visit creates lasting memories.
